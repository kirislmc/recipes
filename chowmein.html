  1. Add 4 cups of water to a boiling pot. Add egg noodles and boil it for 4-6 mins on medium heat.

  2. Once cooked, drain the water and rinse the noodles under cold water. Add oil to the noodles and mix well. This will prevent the noodles from getting overcooked and becoming mushy.

  3. Heat the pan. Add oil to the pan. Add the chicken and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.

  4. Next, add the onion and saute for a few minutes. Then add the vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are cooked but still slightly crunchy.

  5. Then add the vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes until they are cooked but still slightly crunchy.

  6. Add all spices and seasoning. Combine everything well.

  7. Add the cooked noodles and soy sauce. Mix all ingredients well and stir-fry for a few minutes.

  8. Finally, garnish it with a spring onion and salt if needed. Serve hot.
