Chicken Momo Recipe

Back to Homepage

Eqipment:

  • a large bowl
  • food processor can be helpful to finely chop aromatics
  • meat grinder or food processor if making your own ground chicken
  • bamboo or metal steamer

Ingredients:

  • Dumpling wrappers: You can make dumpling wrapper from scratch or use store-bought kind.
  • Ground chicken
  • Aromatics like onions, ginger, garlic, cilantro
  • Spices such as Momo masala
  • Ghee, butter, or oil

Here we go again to the cooking process!

  1. Use a store-bought dumpling wrapper or make the momo wrapper from scratch.

  2. Next prep the fillings. I'd suggest grinding your own chicken using chicken thigh if possible for best result.

  3. And last step is to wrap the dumplings. You can choose your desired shape but the crescent moon and money purse are two popular momo shapes in Nepal.

  4. You can cook momo via steaming (most popular and my preferred method) or pan frying them. You can also freeze them for later use.

  5. Serve Hot and enjoy!
